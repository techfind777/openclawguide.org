What is Cursor?

Cursor is a AI code editor that has gained popularity in the AI community. Its main strength: excellent code generation. However, it has limitations: ide-only, not a general agent platform.

What is OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is an open-source AI agent platform with 150K+ GitHub stars. It lets you deploy AI agents on your own server with full control over personality (via SOUL.md), multi-channel communication, persistent memory, and extensible skills.

Key Differences

1. Personality & Customization

OpenClaw's SOUL.md system gives your agent a real personality ‚Äî expertise, communication style, boundaries, and workflow patterns. Cursor doesn't have an equivalent personality system.

2. Self-Hosting & Privacy

OpenClaw runs on your own server. Your data never leaves your infrastructure. Cursor is cloud-only ‚Äî your data is processed on their servers.

3. Multi-Channel Communication

OpenClaw natively supports 10+ channels: Slack, Discord, Telegram, WhatsApp, email, and more. Your agent is available wherever your users are. Cursor typically works through a single interface.

4. Cost

OpenClaw is free and open source. You only pay for hosting ($5-20/month for a VPS). Cursor charges $20-200+/month depending on usage.
