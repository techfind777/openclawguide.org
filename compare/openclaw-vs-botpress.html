What is Botpress?

Botpress is a chatbot platform that has gained popularity in the AI community. Its main strength: visual flow builder, nlu engine. However, it has limitations: chatbot-focused, not a full agent platform.

What is OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is an open-source AI agent platform with 150K+ GitHub stars. It lets you deploy AI agents on your own server with full control over personality (via SOUL.md), multi-channel communication, persistent memory, and extensible skills.

Key Differences

1. Personality & Customization

OpenClaw's SOUL.md system gives your agent a real personality — expertise, communication style, boundaries, and workflow patterns. Botpress doesn't have an equivalent personality system.

2. Self-Hosting & Privacy

OpenClaw runs on your own server. Your data never leaves your infrastructure. Botpress can also be self-hosted, but OpenClaw offers a more complete agent platform.

3. Multi-Channel Communication

OpenClaw natively supports 10+ channels: Slack, Discord, Telegram, WhatsApp, email, and more. Your agent is available wherever your users are. Botpress has some channel support but is more limited.

4. Cost

OpenClaw is free and open source. You only pay for hosting ($5-20/month for a VPS). Both are open source, but OpenClaw provides a more complete out-of-the-box experience.

When to Choose Botpress

  • Visual flow builder, NLU engine
  • You need chatbot platform specifically
  • You're already invested in the Botpress ecosystem

When to Choose OpenClaw

  • You want full control over your AI agent's personality and behavior
  • You need multi-channel communication (Slack + Discord + Telegram + more)
  • Data privacy matters — everything stays on your server
  • You want persistent memory and context across conversations
  • You need extensible skills and automation capabilities
